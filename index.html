INAFA’MAOLEK

CHamoru concept meaning to “make good” for the benefit of all in the community over independent needs. Taking care of y’all is taking care of ourself. We're not here to follow trends. We show up, do our thing, with skillful expertise and authenticity, because that’s real quality service and quality is what y’all deserve. With a blend of creativity, strategy, and heart, we work towards providing brevity in your day by providing a moment to for you - you can exhale before tackling the day ahead. For us we see every day as an opportunity to learn, grow, and do something meaningful no matter how big or small. Depend on it.

Big Ideas, Real Impact.

In the spirit of Inafa Maolek, Tations 7 Percent Pledge Program is a collaborative effort to reduce food waste and help others live well. Each day we deliver end of the day consumables to those without and donate 7% gross profits to non-profits or mutual aid groups aimed at improving the quality of life of those most vulnerable.Grounded in sustainability and the pursuit of a well lived life, we’re on a mission to make a positive impact. Every day is an opportunity to learn, grow, and do something meaningful. We hope to be and do well
